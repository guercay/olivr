Our Goals

The project aims to modernize agricultural education by integrating Virtual Reality (VR) into training for precision pruning of olive trees, focusing on Arbequina, Arbosana, Koroneiki and Turkish local varieties.

Objectives include improving participants' practical skills, enhancing digital literacy, and promoting sustainable farming practices.

By providing accessible, high-quality training, the project empowers agricultural professionals and students while fostering innovation and environmental sustainability.

🎯

Practical Skills

Improve participants' hands-on pruning techniques through immersive VR simulations.

💻

Digital Literacy

Enhance digital competence of agricultural professionals and students.

🌱

Sustainable Farming

Promote environmentally responsible cultivation and resource-efficient practices.

🤝

Foster cross-border collaboration between partner institutions across Europe.

Olive Varieties

Focused Cultivars

The OliVR training program covers precision pruning techniques for four key olive varieties, each with distinct growth habits and requirements.

🫒

Arbequina

Spanish variety known for its small fruit and high oil content.

🌿

Arbosana

Hardy cultivar with excellent adaptability to intensive farming systems.

☀️

Koroneiki

Greek variety prized for its peppery oil and disease resistance.

Turkish Local

Indigenous Turkish cultivars with unique regional characteristics.
